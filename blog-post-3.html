Choosing the Right Cloud Platform

Select based on your specific needs:

  • AWS: Most mature, broadest service catalog, enterprise focus
  • Azure: Best for Microsoft shops, hybrid cloud, Windows workloads
  • GCP: Strong in data analytics, AI/ML, and Kubernetes

Future Trend: Multi-cloud strategies are becoming the norm, with 85% of enterprises using multiple cloud providers.
